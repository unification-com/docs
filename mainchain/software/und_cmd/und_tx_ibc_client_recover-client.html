# und tx ibc client recover-client

recover an IBC client

# Synopsis

Submit a recover IBC client proposal along with an initial deposit Please specify a subject client identifier you want to recover Please specify the substitute client the subject client will be recovered to.

und tx ibc client recover-client [subject-client-id] [substitute-client-id] [flags]
